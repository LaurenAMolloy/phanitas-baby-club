About Us

“Aiming to create complete people with strong personalities. And above all LOVE, RESPECT, DEDICATION to each child.”

Welcome to the nursery - childcare center for all-day care, employment and study "Phanita's Baby Club". This is a model preschool and education center, where your child will be properly educated, socialized and will be able to gradually integrate into society as a whole more easily. Phanita's Baby Club daycare center was founded in 2007 with all the specifications required by today's standards.

We are located in the area of ​​Agios Sylas, Ypsonas and in between in the area of ​​the new Limassol Hospital and Kolossi. The station's main characteristic is a place of life and education. A space that is not a station for "parking" the child but on the contrary is a space for creation and learning, with a climate of warm human relationships between parents - children - teachers. A space of coexistence, collaboration, creation, sharing, love, offering...

Our Basic Principles :

  • I work in a team
  • Each child has their own uniqueness and individuality.
  • The educational program is based on all the interdisciplinary modules of early childhood education and care, in accordance with education in Cyprus.

Our Goal :

  • Socialization
  • Mutual respect
  • Cooperation
  • Self-esteem
  • Love for learning and creation
  • HAPPY FACES…

Care and Education of Our Little Friends… :

  • Meets the needs for security, affection, love…
  • Recreational activities (teamwork)
  • Development of creativity of expression (mentally and skills)
  • Free and creative play
  • Development of initiative and independence
  • Respect for everyone's cultural and religious identity
  • To be able to socialize with all people (young and old)….
