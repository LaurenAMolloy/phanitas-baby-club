Our 3–4 & 4–5 Year-Old Class: Exploring, Creating, and Growing

In this section, we offer an engaging environment where your child can begin to explore their creativity, learn new skills, and develop a deeper understanding of the world around them. Our activities are designed to nurture their cognitive, physical, and social growth, including:

  • Painting to foster creativity and fine motor skills
  • Working with clay to enhance tactile exploration and hand-eye coordination
  • Adhesive crafts to build fine motor skills and creativity
  • Fairy tales – audiovisual stories from CDs and DVDs to spark imagination
  • Puppet shows that encourage storytelling and imaginative play
  • Music with movement to develop rhythm, coordination, and expression
  • Gymnastics and dance to enhance physical strength, flexibility, and balance
  • Poems that develop language and listening skills
  • Introduction to basic cuisine skills through fun cooking activities
  • Basic English to begin language development and communication
  • Computer activities to encourage basic digital literacy and learning
  • Educational and free play games that promote critical thinking and creativity
  • Imitation games that support social and cognitive development
  • Portfolio creation to track progress and encourage self-expression
  • Early writing activities to develop literacy skills
  • Books to foster a love of reading and storytelling
  • And many more seasonal activities designed to keep your child engaged and learning
